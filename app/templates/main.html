Who We Are

The SimLE Scientific Association is a group of talented and ambitious students from the Gdańsk University of Technology, united primarily by our desire to create.

We believe that by working together in interdisciplinary teams, we can reach for the stars, venture where no one has gone before, and do daily what our peers might consider impossible.

As part of our circle, we implement programs and projects that bring together students from various disciplines, faculties, and even universities. We create space, tools, and conditions – everything necessary for young people to experience science in practice, pursue their passions, and discover their potential.
